About


HACK4BENGAL began with the idea of bringing together the best of the best in the field of technology and provide a platform for the students to showcase their skills and work.

  • Get the feel of a Physical Hackathon even Digitally
  • Get rewarded for the work you do, and always win!
  • More than 6 months of planning, and here we are

Sister Nivedita University, Deptartment of Computer Science and Engineering, Technical Committee was created to deal with all the technical Activities, Events, Clubs, Technical Chapters within the University. It has over 15 Chapters. Active 50+ Core Members and 1500+ Participating Members.
